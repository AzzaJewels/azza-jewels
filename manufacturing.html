Our State-of-the-Art Atelier

At Azza Jewels, we control every facet of creation in our 12,000 sq ft Muscat facility. From precision CAD modeling to hand-carving wax prototypes, castings, stone-setting, and finishing, our masters never leave their bench —ensuring the highest level of quality and accountability at every stage.

50–60 Artisans

Each with decades of experience, our team includes master goldsmiths, gem-setters, engravers, and polishers—all dedicated to perfection.

12,000 sq ft Facility

Equipped with both traditional benches and CNC-aided workstations, blending heritage techniques with modern precision.

Quality & Compliance

Every finished piece undergoes our seven-point inspection—density tests, gemstone certification, and finish verification—before it leaves the atelier.
