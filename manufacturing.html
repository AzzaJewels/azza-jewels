Uncompromising Quality Control

Every masterpiece bearing the Azza Jewels name is more than beautiful—it’s guaranteed to meet the highest international benchmarks for purity, durability, and craftsmanship. From raw gold to finished heirloom, our multi-stage quality assurance protocol leaves nothing to chance.

  • ISO-Grade Assay Testing: All gold alloys are analyzed in our on-site, ISO-accredited lab using fire assay and XRF spectrometry to confirm a minimum 91.6% gold content (916 hallmark).
  • Dedicated QC Department: A specialized team inspects every component—verifying weight tolerance (±0.02 g), surface finish, and solder integrity—before moving to the next stage.
  • Government-Approved Hallmarking: Prior to any sale in The Sultanate of Oman, each item is officially stamped by the Precious Metal Assaying Laboratory at the Directorate General for Specifications and Metrology, certifying its purity and authenticity.
  • Global Standards Compliance: We adhere to ISO 9202, BIS 610, and ASTM F75 for casting alloys and gemstone grading, ensuring your jewelry passes rigorous international quality audits.
  • Uncompromising Quality & Assurance: Before export, every piece is hallmarked and released directly from our ISO-accredited lab.
  • Lifetime Performance Guarantee: Beyond initial testing, we offer free maintenance and re-assaying services, so your piece remains as flawless years from now as the day you received it.
