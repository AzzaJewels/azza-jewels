About Azza Jewels & Mr. Chetan Shah

Azza Jewels is a premier luxury jewelry house founded by Mr. Chetan Shah, a seasoned entrepreneur with three decades of expertise. From expanding India’s Maa Padma Exports & Shri Pannalal Jewellers to launching Cambay Gold FZE in Sharjah, his vision has always fused heritage with innovation.

Legacy in India

Under his leadership, exports surpassed ₹10 billion annually, generating €450 million between 2017–2022 through strategic collaborations and world-class craftsmanship.

Global Expansion

Settling in the UAE, he founded Cambay Gold FZE—leveraging Dubai’s infrastructure to deliver exquisite designs worldwide.

Current Ventures

Now at the helm of Kanak Gold, he continues to elevate quality and drive operational excellence in the global jewellery sector.
