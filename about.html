About Azza Jewels & Mr. Chetan Shah

Azza Jewels is a premier luxury jewelry house founded by Mr. Chetan Shah, a seasoned entrepreneur with three decades of expertise. From expanding India’s Maa Padma Exports & Shri Pannalal Jewellers to launching Cambay Gold FZE in Sharjah, his vision has always fused heritage with innovation.

Legacy in India

Under his leadership, exports grew substantially through strategic collaborations and world-class craftsmanship, firmly establishing our reputation on the global stage.

Global Expansion

Settling in the UAE, he founded Cambay Gold FZE—leveraging Dubai’s legacy of handmade jewellery to deliver exquisite designs worldwide.

Current Ventures

Now at the helm of Kanak Gold, he continues to elevate quality and drive operational excellence in the global jewellery sector.

Global Reach Through Strategic Alliances

By leveraging Oman’s preferential partnerships, Azza Jewels brings its handcrafted heritage to discerning customers worldwide.

Omani Heritage Hub

Rooted in the Sultanate of Oman, our Muscat atelier fuses centuries-old craftsmanship with modern precision—creating treasures that embody our nation’s storied legacy.

United States

U.S.–Oman Free Trade Agreement
In effect since 2009, this landmark pact removes tariffs on fine jewelry, allowing us to bring our luxury creations to American markets with exceptional value.

Gulf Cooperation Council

As an integral GCC member, Oman participates in a unified market that extends tariff-free access to key partners like Singapore, the EFTA (Switzerland, Norway, Iceland, Liechtenstein), and New Zealand—broadening our regional footprint.

Pan-Arab Access

Through the Greater Arab Free Trade Area (GAFTA), our artisans’ work flows seamlessly across most Arab League nations, celebrating shared culture and craftsmanship throughout the region.

Together with our deep roots in Oman’s local heritage market, these alliances empower Azza Jewels to deliver heirloom-quality pieces wherever elegance is cherished.
