About Azza Jewels

Azza Jewels is a premier luxury jewelry house founded by our visionary Mr. Chetan Shah, a seasoned entrepreneur with three decades of expertise. From expanding India’s Maa Padma Exports & Shri Pannalal Jewellers to launching Cambay Gold FZE in Sharjah, his vision has always fused heritage with innovation.

Legacy in India

Under his leadership, exports grew substantially through strategic collaborations and world-class craftsmanship, firmly establishing our reputation on the global stage.

Global Expansion

Settling in the UAE, he founded Cambay Gold FZE—leveraging Dubai’s legacy of handmade jewellery to deliver exquisite designs worldwide.

Current Ventures

Now heading Azza Jewels, he continues to elevate quality and drive operational excellence in the global jewellery sector.

We extend our heartfelt gratitude to the Sultanate of Oman—whose rich heritage and visionary support shape every facet of our craft. Your inspiration empowers us to forge timeless masterpieces that honor our shared legacy.

Global Reach Through Alliances

By leveraging Oman’s preferential partnerships, Azza Jewels brings its handcrafted heritage to discerning customers worldwide.

Omani Heritage Hub

Rooted in the Sultanate of Oman, our Muscat atelier fuses centuries-old craftsmanship with modern precision—creating treasures that embody our nation’s storied legacy.

United States

U.S.–Oman Free Trade Agreement
Since 2009, this pact removes tariffs on fine jewelry, letting us bring luxury creations to the U.S. with exceptional value.

Gulf Cooperation Council

As a GCC member, Oman enjoys tariff-free access to partners like Singapore, EFTA (Switzerland, Norway, Iceland, Liechtenstein), and New Zealand—broadening our regional footprint.

Pan-Arab Access

Through GAFTA, our artisans’ work flows across most Arab League nations, celebrating shared culture and craftsmanship.

Together with Oman’s local heritage market, these alliances empower Azza Jewels to deliver heirloom-quality pieces wherever elegance is cherished.
