About Our Founder

Mr. Chetan Shah, the visionary founder of Azza Jewels, brings over three decades of excellence in the gems & jewellery industry. Read More →

  • 1998: Began career trading fine metals across the Middle East.
  • 2006: Founded first jewellery manufacturing unit in India.
  • 2015: Launched Azza Jewels, merging heritage with cutting-edge technology.
  • 2017–2022: Led exports of ₹115.5 billion and generated ₹450 million in revenue with Maa Padma Exports & Shri Pannalal Jewellers.
  • 2023: Established Cambay Gold FZE in Sharjah, UAE, to expand global reach.
  • 2024: Assumed management of Kanak Gold, strengthening its market leadership.
  • 2025 – Present: Azza Jewels operates from its state-of-the-art Oman facility, continually crafting new masterpieces for clients worldwide.
