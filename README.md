## About The Project

This repository contains the source code for the **official website of Azza Jewels**, a luxury jewelry manufacturer proudly based in Oman.

The website was designed to reflect the brand’s commitment to **heritage craftsmanship, premium quality, and refined aesthetics**. Through elegant design and carefully curated visuals, the platform showcases Azza Jewels’ collections, manufacturing capabilities, and brand story.

Every element of the site—from typography to animations—was crafted to convey the **luxury identity of the brand** while ensuring a seamless and responsive user experience across all devices.

---

## Brand Philosophy

Azza Jewels embodies the fusion of **traditional Omani artistry and modern jewelry innovation**.

Operating from a **16,000 sq ft production facility in Oman**, the brand specializes in crafting fine gold jewelry in **18K, 21K, and 22K carats**, combining advanced manufacturing technology with the expertise of master artisans.

The company is proudly recognized with the **Made in Oman certification**, affirming its commitment to authentic local manufacturing and excellence.
